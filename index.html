Accessibility

  • I want everyone to be able to use this site, including people who rely on assistive technology.

What are we doing?

  • This site aims to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 at Level AA. It uses semantic landmarks and headings, works fully from the keyboard, keeps focus inside dialogs and returns it when they close, labels every icon and image, respects your system “reduce motion” setting, and supports browser zoom and text resizing.

How are we doing?

  • Most of the experience meets that bar. Because the design recreates the look of iOS 6, a few labels and secondary lines keep the original low-contrast gray styling and may not reach AA contrast — that's a deliberate aesthetic choice. All content stays readable with browser zoom, and every control is keyboard- and screen-reader-accessible. I keep working to improve it.
